Payment FAQs

Common Questions About Paying

How do I know how much to pay?
Your counsellor will tell you the exact course fee and any applicable discounts during or after your free demo session. Do not pay before confirming the amount with your counsellor at +91 73036 09096.
Is Razorpay safe to use?
Yes. Razorpay is India's most trusted payment gateway, used by 10M+ businesses. All transactions are 256-bit SSL encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant. Your card details are handled entirely by Razorpay — never stored on our servers.
Can I pay in instalments?
Yes. We offer payment in 2–3 instalments for most courses. EMI via credit card (0% or low-cost) is also available through Razorpay. Contact us at +91 73036 09096 to set up an instalment plan before making your first payment.
What do I do after paying?
Take a screenshot of the Razorpay payment confirmation and WhatsApp it to +91 73036 09096. We'll confirm your seat, share batch details, and give you access to the student portal and study materials within 24 hours.
What is the refund policy?
Refund requests made within 7 days of payment and before the second live session are eligible for a full refund minus the registration fee. After that, a partial refund may be considered on a case-by-case basis. See our full Refund Policy for details.
I paid but didn't receive confirmation. What should I do?
WhatsApp your Razorpay payment screenshot to +91 73036 09096 immediately. Our team will manually verify and confirm within 2 hours during working hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–9 PM).
