Data Analytics — FAQs

Is any prior knowledge required for this course?
No. This course starts from basics. If you can use a computer and work with files, you can start this course. No coding or math background required.
What is the difference between Data Analytics and Data Analytics with Python?
Data Analytics covers Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau — no coding. Data Analytics with Python adds Python, Pandas, NumPy, Statistics, and EDA on top. Choose Python if you want deeper analysis and higher salary potential (₹4–12 LPA vs ₹3–8 LPA).
Will this course help me get a job without prior work experience?
Yes. Many of our fresh graduates land MIS Executive and junior analyst roles directly after this course. The capstone projects act as proof of skill during interviews.

Expect 10–15 hours per week for online batches (live sessions + practice). Offline batches are typically 1.5–2 hours per session, 3 days a week, with assignments in between.
