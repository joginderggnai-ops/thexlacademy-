Common Questions

Tableau Training — FAQs

Do I need to know statistics or coding before this course?
No. Tableau is a visual tool — no coding required. Basic Excel familiarity is helpful but not mandatory.
What is the difference between Tableau and Power BI?
Both are BI tools. Tableau has superior visualisation flexibility and is preferred at analytics consulting firms and product companies. Power BI is more common in enterprise India. Many analysts learn both.
Can I learn Tableau for free with Tableau Public?
Tableau Public is free and works for all course content. Tableau Desktop is available on a free student trial. You'll publish your portfolio dashboards to Tableau Public.
Is Tableau still relevant given Power BI's growth?
Yes. Tableau is standard at top analytics firms (Mu Sigma, Fractal, WNS) and in global consulting (KPMG, Accenture, McKinsey). Many job descriptions list Tableau + Power BI together.
How do I showcase Tableau skills to employers?
You'll publish both capstone dashboards to Tableau Public, creating a live portfolio link you can share on LinkedIn and your resume. The instructor will guide the portfolio and LinkedIn optimisation session.
