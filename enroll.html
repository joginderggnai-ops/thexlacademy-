Enrolment FAQs

Common Questions Before You Enroll

When does the next batch start?
New batches start every month. Once you submit your enrollment form, our counsellor will call you within 24 hours and tell you the exact date of the upcoming batch for your preferred course and timing slot.
Is there a free trial or demo class?
Yes — every student gets a free demo class before they need to pay anything. The demo is a live session (online or at the branch) where you experience the teaching style, meet the faculty, and ask questions. Only after the demo do you confirm your seat.
What payment options are available?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and bank transfer via our Razorpay payment link. We also offer EMI plans — contact us for details. Education loan assistance is also available.
Can I switch from online to offline or vice versa after enrollment?
Yes, subject to seat availability in the batch. Contact your assigned counsellor within the first 2 weeks to make the switch. Most students who switch do so for scheduling reasons — both modes are fully equivalent in curriculum.
What happens if I miss classes?
All live sessions are recorded. Missed classes can be caught up via recordings. You can also attend equivalent sessions in the next batch to fill gaps — our faculty support ensures no student falls behind.
