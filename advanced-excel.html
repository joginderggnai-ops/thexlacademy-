Advanced Excel — FAQs

I already know basic Excel. Is this course still useful?
Yes. If you're comfortable with SUM, VLOOKUP, and basic formatting, you're right at the start level. This course covers everything that hiring managers mean when they write "Advanced Excel" in a job description.
Which Excel version do you teach?
We teach on Microsoft Excel 2019 and Excel 365. XLOOKUP and dynamic arrays are covered — if you're on an older version, we show compatibility alternatives.
Is VBA / Macros difficult to learn?
We cover VBA at a practical level — recording macros, editing them, and writing simple loops. You don't need programming experience. The goal is to automate repetitive tasks, not to become a developer.
Will Advanced Excel help me get a job?
Advanced Excel alone qualifies you for MIS Executive and junior analyst roles (₹3–5 LPA). Combine it with SQL and Power BI to significantly expand your options and salary band.
How long are the live sessions?
Sessions are 1.5–2 hours, typically 3 days per week. The 4-week course runs at a focused pace — expect 10–15 hours of live instruction plus assignments and project work.
