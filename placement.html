Placement FAQs

Common Questions About Our Placement Program

Is placement guaranteed?
We provide full placement assistance — resume building, mock interviews, and direct company connects. While no institute can legally guarantee a specific job (as hiring decisions rest with the employer), our 95% placement rate reflects how effective our process is. We continue supporting you until you are placed.
How long does placement typically take after course completion?
Most students are placed within 30–90 days of course completion. Students who complete their projects, attend mock interviews, and actively participate in placement drives are placed faster. The average for a fresher is 60–75 days.
Do you help working professionals switch careers, not just freshers?
Yes — and career switchers often get better packages because they bring domain knowledge from their previous industry. Our placement team pitches your prior experience alongside your new analytics skills as a unique combination.
Which cities do you have placement connections in?
Our strongest placement networks are in Delhi NCR (Delhi, Noida, Gurugram), Bangalore, Pune, and Mumbai. We also support remote and hybrid placements for online students across India.
Can I see examples of placed students' job profiles?
Yes — WhatsApp us at +91 73036 09096 and we'll share anonymised placement records from the past 2 years, including company names, roles, and salary bands relevant to the course you're interested in.
Do online students get the same placement support?
Yes. Online students get identical resume building, LinkedIn profile, mock interviews, and placement drive participation. Remote and hybrid roles are also included in our placement drives alongside on-site positions.
