🚇 How to Reach Us

  • 1By Metro (Blue Line) — Take the Blue Line to Dwarka Sector-13 station. Use Exit 1. The building is directly opposite the metro gate — 2-minute walk.
  • 2By Bus — DTC buses to Dwarka Sec-13 stop right outside. Routes 764, 764-A, and 795 serve this stop from Central Delhi.

Common Questions

Delhi Centre — FAQs

Is this the main/headquarters campus?
Yes. Dwarka Sec-13 is The XL Academy's founding campus and headquarters. It has our senior-most instructors, the most batch options, and the strongest placement network.
Is parking available at the Delhi campus?
Yes. The YC Co-Working Space complex has parking for two-wheelers and four-wheelers. However, we recommend coming by metro as the centre is literally opposite Dwarka Sec-13 station.
Can I switch between offline and online if I miss a class?
Yes. If you miss an offline session, you can attend the corresponding online session and access recorded material. Flexibility is built into our program design.
Are there demo classes I can attend before enrolling?
Absolutely. We run regular free demo classes both online and at the Delhi campus. Book through the form on this page and our team will schedule one at your preferred time.
What is the nearest metro station?
Dwarka Sector-13 Metro Station on the Blue Line (Noida / Vaishali ↔ Dwarka Sector-21). The building is directly visible from Exit 1 — a 2-minute walk.
