Why Study in Pune

India's Emerging IT Hub — Great Opportunity, Lower Cost

Pune's IT sector is growing faster than most Indian cities — with MNC campuses, a strong automotive analytics market, and a large fresher absorption rate.

🏭

MNC Campus Cluster

Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini, and many Fortune 500 companies have large campuses in Hinjewadi — creating a steady flow of analytics, MIS, and reporting job openings.

🚗

Auto + Manufacturing Analytics

Pune is India's automotive capital — home to Tata Motors, Bajaj Auto, Force Motors, and their supply chains. Manufacturing analytics and operational MIS are high-demand roles unique to Pune.

🎓

College Freshers Hub

Pune has one of India's largest concentrations of engineering and commerce graduates. Our courses are built to absorb freshers and place them quickly in their first analytics role.

💼

Work–Life Balance City

Pune offers competitive salaries with much lower living costs than Bangalore or Mumbai — making it ideal for professionals who want to grow their careers without burning out.

Infosys BPO, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini, Bajaj Auto, Tata Motors, and 100+ IT and manufacturing companies in Hinjewadi, Baner, Kharadi, and Magarpatta hire analytics and MIS professionals regularly.

Yes — especially for freshers. Pune absorbs a large number of entry-level analytics hires every year. The cost of living is lower than Bangalore and Mumbai, and salaries are competitive for the experience level.
Is Hinjewadi accessible from the centre?
Yes. Hinjewadi IT Park is one of Pune's biggest job zones and is accessible from Baner/Aundh by auto or cab. Many of our students have placed into Hinjewadi companies directly from Pune placements.
