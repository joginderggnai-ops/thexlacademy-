Loan FAQs

Common Questions About Education Loans

Do I need a guarantor or collateral for a loan?
For most of our courses (which fall under ₹4 lakh), loans are available without collateral. A parent or guardian may be required as a co-applicant by some lenders. Our team will guide you based on your specific loan partner options.
Is the 0% EMI really interest-free?
Yes — 0% EMI via Razorpay is genuinely interest-free for eligible credit card holders. Available on HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, and SBI credit cards for 3, 6, or 12-month tenures. Your bank may charge a processing fee of ₹0–500 depending on your card.
When do I start repaying the loan?
For NBFC education loans, repayment typically begins 1–3 months after course completion — by which time most of our students are already placed and earning. The exact moratorium period depends on the loan partner.
What if I don't get placed — do I still need to repay?
Education loans are independent of placement outcomes — the loan agreement is between you and the lending institution, not XL Academy. However, our 95% placement rate means the vast majority of students are earning within 3 months of completing the course.
How quickly can a loan be approved?
NBFC and fintech partner loans are typically approved in 24–72 business hours once complete documentation is submitted. Instalment plans with XL Academy directly are set up same-day after a conversation with your counsellor.
